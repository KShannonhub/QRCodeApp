Privacy Policy for Cubey Scans

Last Updated: May 18, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Cubey Scans. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Cubey Scans does NOT collect, store, or share any personal information or data from its users. Specifically:

  • No Personal Data: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personal identifiers.
  • No QR Code Data: All QR codes scanned or generated are processed locally on your device. We do not store, transmit, or have access to any QR code content.
  • No Location Data: We do not collect or track your location.
  • No Device Information: We do not collect device identifiers, IP addresses, or hardware information.

Third-Party Services

Our app may display advertisements through Google AdMob. These third-party services may collect anonymized data for advertising purposes as described in their own privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy

We do not control these third-party services or their data collection practices.

Permissions

Cubey Scans requires the following permissions to function:

  • Camera Permission: Required to scan QR codes. The camera is only accessed when you actively use the scanning feature.
  • Internet Permission: Used only for displaying ads through Google AdMob.

Data Security

Since we do not collect or store any personal data, there is no user data to secure on our servers. All QR code operations happen locally on your device.

Children's Privacy

Cubey Scans does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age. Our app does not target children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
